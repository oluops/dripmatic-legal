How to Use Dripmatiq

A 6-step walkthrough — from first launch to your first AI-styled outfit.

In this guide
  1. Open the app & complete onboarding
  2. Build your closet
  3. Add a selfie for FitMatic
  4. Generate an outfit
  5. Try it on with FitMatic
  6. Save and revisit favorites

1 Open the app & complete onboarding

A quick 3-screen setup teaches the AI your style baseline.

What you'll set

  • Style archetype — Classic, Streetwear, Minimal, etc.
  • Body type & gender expression — for accurate fit on FitMatic
  • Color preferences — what you love and what to avoid
Tip: You can change any of this later in the 🎨 STYLE tab.

2 Build your closet

Add real garments so the AI styles outfits from clothes you actually own.

How to add an item

  1. Tap the 👗 CLOSET tab
  2. Pick the right category at the top (Tops, Bottoms, Shoes…)
  3. Tap the gold + button
  4. Snap or pick a photo, then fill in color, fabric, and brand
Why photos matter: when an item has a photo, FitMatic uses the real garment for try-on instead of an AI guess — colors and fabric look exactly like yours.
New: photos now sync to your account, so they survive reinstalls and switching phones.

3 Add a selfie for FitMatic

Optional — but lets the try-on look like you, not a stock model.

Where to add it

  1. Open the 🔥 TODAY tab
  2. Scroll to the FitMatic card
  3. Tap "Add selfie" and pick a clear, front-facing photo

Best results with a well-lit headshot — eyes open, no heavy filters, plain background if possible.

Privacy: your selfie stays in your private account and is only sent to the AI render service when you tap FitMatic. See the Privacy Policy.

4 Generate an outfit

Two ways to ask the AI for a new look.

Two ways to generate

  • 📱 Shake your phone — fastest way to roll a new look
  • ✨ Tap the gold button on the Today tab

Each outfit considers your local weather, the time of day, and the occasion you've selected.

Need a different vibe? Tap the occasion chip (Casual / Work / Date / Formal) at the top to re-roll for that context.

5 Try it on with FitMatic

See the outfit rendered on a model — or on you, if you added a selfie.

How it works

  1. Generate an outfit on the Today tab
  2. Tap the 🪞 FITMATIC button
  3. Wait ~15–30 seconds while the AI renders the look

If your outfit includes sunglasses, FitMatic now correctly swaps any glasses you're wearing in your selfie for the requested shades — without changing your face.

Pro & Elite plans: get higher render quality, more monthly try-ons, and the ability to chain multiple garments per render.

6 Save and revisit favorites

Build a personal lookbook of outfits that worked.

Saving an outfit

  1. Tap the heart or SAVE button on any generated look
  2. Find it later in the ❤️ SAVED tab
  3. Tap any saved outfit to re-render with FitMatic or share
Mark as worn: tap "I wore this" so the AI learns what you reach for and stops repeating fits too often.

★ Pro tips

Small habits that make Dripmatiq much smarter.

  • Add at least 3 tops, 2 bottoms, and 1 pair of shoes before generating — the AI needs a minimum set to mix and match.
  • Take photos in natural light against a plain background so colors register accurately.
  • Use the Style tab to toggle weather, season, and occasion as factors in outfit generation.
  • Don't like a suggestion? Just shake again — every roll is free. The AI never repeats the exact same outfit twice in a row.
  • Going on a trip? Set a destination on the Today tab to plan looks for the local weather there.
